About us

Welcome to our Purr-fect Paws and Boba Shop, a haven where you can enjoy the purr-fect fusion of cats and boba. Originating from Taiwan, the concept of a cat cafe has grown globally, offering a unique blend of relaxation and feline companionship. Our cafe is more than just a place to enjoy a refreshing boba drink; it's a sanctuary where you can unwind, connect with others, and meet our friendly feline companions. Our cafe is also a foster home for rescue cats, providing them with a safe and loving environment. We believe that our cafe offers a mutually beneficial relationship for both cats and humans. For the cats, it provides a safe place to socialize and find potential new homes. For humans, it provides a break from everyday life, a chance to relax, and the opportunity to meet and bond with these adorable creatures. Whether you're a boba lover seeking a cozy spot to enjoy your favorite drink or a cat enthusiast looking for a place to meet and interact with cats, our cafe offers something for everyone. We pride ourselves on our commitment to providing a comfortable, welcoming environment where everyone feels at home. At our Purr-fect Paws and Boba Shop, we strive to create a community centered around love for cats and the joy of boba. Join us and experience a day filled with purrs, sips, and laughter. Welcome to the purr-fect fusion of cats and boba!
